Full Animation: The End Product

And here it is! The final animation, titled The Marble

There are definitely some things to improve on though. For one thing, glass doesn’t exactly crack as I drew it. For another, the music is just.. It’s not good. I ended up running out of time towards the end, so I ended up using default music from iMovie and Premiere Rush. Also the fps for most of my animation is (if I remember correctly) 6 fps, and the usual rates are typically in the double digits (although the main reason was the overwhelming time pressure). So that’s also something to keep in mind in terms of how this project went down.

Overall though, I’m happy with how everything turned out. The final animation is pretty good considering I was (and still am) a complete beginner, and it’s something I’m proud to show to others.

And that’s it! Thanks for making it this far! I wrote a lot more than I intended since this project was mainly to practice my coding skills. Till next time!
