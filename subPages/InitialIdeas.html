Initial Ideas

First, a little about the general school project this was created for. The project was a requirement for all gr 12s to complete, and involved having a mentor, self-learning and completing a presentation about the final project and the journey we took. We worked on developing several soft skills through that journey and generally experienced the process that setting, working towards and achieving a goal could take.

I took this opportunity to delve into making a full animation with a storyline. I had done a couple of smaller animations on my iPad, mostly to explore the software and see what animating on it was like.

Birth of the Marbles

And some random doodles led to these marbles being born:

Combine that with the main project, and some ideas began to form. (Also marbles would be pretty easy to animate. Because, well, they’re round and I wouldn’t have to worry about limbs)

My initial ideas included a short, but well meaning storyline that left a good message without physical words. (Brainstorming included a lot of watching shorts on YouTube)

And after a couple of scrapped ideas, I landed on having two marbles (based on those doodles before) as the main characters. One would get cracked somehow, and wouldn’t be too excited about the new imperfections. Then sunlight, somehow, somewhere would shine on the marble and, well, light through a cracked glass marble is actually really pretty. And that’s it. That’s the general idea process leading to my animation.
